How to Play
1. Create or Join a Lobby

One player creates a lobby and shares the room code (or invite link). Everyone else joins on their own phone. You need at least 3 players to start a round.

2. Phase 1 — Prompt & Vote

Everyone gets a question on their screen, like "Who is the messiest?" — except the secretly chosen Odd1Owt, who gets a completely different question. Nobody knows who that is. Everyone votes on who best fits their own question.

3. Phase 2 — Interrogation

The real question is revealed to the whole group. Now you talk it out — ask each other questions, defend your vote, and try to sniff out who was answering something totally different. The Odd1Owt has to bluff their way through without giving it away.

4. Phase 3 — Final Vote

Everyone locks in one final vote for who they think the Odd1Owt is. Votes stay hidden until everybody has voted.

5. Phase 4 — The Reveal

The group's top suspect is revealed, followed by the verdict: CAUGHT if they nailed the Odd1Owt, or ESCAPED if the Odd1Owt got away with it. Both questions are shown side by side, scores update, and you're straight into the next round.

Tip

The host can pick a question deck before starting — Casual, Spicy, Deep Cut, Random, or All Decks mixed together. Switch it up between rounds to keep things fresh.
