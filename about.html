About

Odd1Owt is a real-time multiplayer party game built for groups who like a bit of chaos. Think Among Us meets Most Likely To: everyone in the room gets a prompt and votes on who fits it best — except one secret player, the Odd1Owt, who's silently been given a different question entirely.

Nobody knows who the Odd1Owt is until the group interrogates each other, casts a final vote, and the truth comes out in a dramatic reveal. Get it right and the group scores. Get it wrong and the Odd1Owt walks free.

Why we built it

Odd1Owt started as a way to bring the energy of "most likely to" games to group hangouts, pres, and society socials — without needing a physical card deck or a moderator reading out prompts. Everything runs live in the browser: create a lobby, share a code, and play from your phones.

What's inside

Five question decks — Casual, Casual 2, Spicy, Deep Cut, and Random — ranging from light-hearted to properly chaotic, plus an "All Decks" mode that mixes everything together. New decks and features get added based on feedback from real games.
