Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Odd1Owt ("we", "the game", "the site") collects, how it's used, and your choices. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

Name and gameplay data: When you join a lobby, the name you enter, your votes, and your score are stored temporarily in our database (Google Firebase Firestore) so the game can run in real time for everyone in your lobby. This data is tied to a random device identifier, not your real identity, and is used only to run the current game session.

Local device storage: We use your browser's local storage to remember your name and unlocked question decks between visits, so you don't have to re-enter them each time. This stays on your device and is not shared with us.

We do not collect: email addresses, passwords, or any personal contact information, unless you contact us directly (see our Contact page).

Cookies & Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its advertising partners use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites, and to measure ad performance.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from sites that use its services, and manage your ad personalization settings, at policies.google.com/technologies/ads. You can also opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on someone's past visits to this website or other websites.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for real-time game data and GitHub Pages for hosting. Both providers may collect standard technical data (such as IP address and browser type) as part of operating their infrastructure. See Firebase's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

Odd1Owt is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top.
